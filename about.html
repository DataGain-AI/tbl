Decade of Progress

Our Growth Milestones

From a localized road transport agency to an intermodal global logistics node. Here is our story of expansion.

2015

Initial Launch

Founded T Balaji Logistics with a single cargo fulfillment terminal in Mumbai and a fleet of 5 heavy transport trucks focusing on state-wide deliveries.

2018

Technological Integration

Equipped our line-haul trailers with cellular GPS tracking, launched a automated transport dashboard, and opened our first warehousing center.

2021

Multi-Hub Expansion

Opened regional dispatch terminals in New Delhi NCR, Chennai, Kolkata, and Bengaluru. Fleet size expanded to 100+ active trucks, securing inter-state operations.

2024

International Entry

Obtained air cargo consolidation licenses and customs broker status at major Indian ocean docks, allowing integrated intermodal ocean transport.

2026

Modern Automation

Integrating autonomous warehouse stock management systems, AI freight route optimization algorithms, and digital cargo APIs for seamless partner tracking.
